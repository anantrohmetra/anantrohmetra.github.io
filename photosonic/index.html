Inspired by the ANS Synthesizer, Oramics, and Kandinsky's Synesthesia

How It Works

  • Spatial (ANS) Mode: Vertical position determines pitch (higher = higher pitch). Inspired by the Soviet ANS synthesizer that read drawings as sound.
  • Kandinsky Mode: Colors determine timbre - reds are harsh sawtooth waves, blues are smooth sine waves, greens are square waves.
  • Gestural Mode: Your drawing gesture becomes a melody - plays back your strokes as musical sequences.
