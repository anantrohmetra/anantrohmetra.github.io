Livecode.blr

The duo explored two generative algorithms sending MIDI to multiple digital and analog synths and drum machines. One is a Markov chain inspired rhythm generator and the other is a recursive melody generator based on the rules of a selected Indian classical raga. This output drives feedback noise from a mixer, while driving an audiovisual system — an unnamed ode to Perlin noise — controlled by a Leap Motion sensor.

Sonically, the set moves through noise, field recordings, and polyrhythmic structures. It explores chaos and unpredictability as structural elements, where improvisation and feedback loops become a part of the composition.
